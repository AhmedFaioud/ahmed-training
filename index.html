🩺 Anal Fissure — Breathe!

  • Exhale on exertion — never hold your breath on heavy reps
  • Brace inward (draw in belly), not outward
  • Trap bar deadlift > conventional (less pressure)
  • If you feel "pressure" → too heavy, reduce load
  • No 1RM testing, stay at RIR 1-3 on strength

💧 Kidney Stone — Hydrate

  • 3L+ water every day
  • 🍋 Lemon water (½ lemon) every morning
  • Sip 500ml during training
  • Normal calcium from food (binds oxalates)
  • Avoid: excess sodium, high-dose vit C supplements

🦵 Left Knee — Warm Up

  • Always do the knee warm-up (see workout tab)
  • Squat to parallel only — go deeper gradually if no pain
  • Prefer leg press if squat bothers you
  • Glute exercises (hip thrust, RDL) = better knee tracking
  • If it hurts → substitute with the alt exercise

🥗 The 5 Simple Rules

  1. Protein in every meal — eggs, meat, chicken, fish, laban, whey
  2. Plate method — Training days: ½ carbs. Rest days: ¼ carbs.
  3. Cut junk — No soda. Fried food ≤1x/wk. Sweets ≤2x/wk.
  4. Olive oil daily — 1-2 tbsp. Don't fear healthy fats.
  5. 3L water + lemon water morning (kidney stone!)

🍽️ Training Day (M/W/F)

  • ☀️ Breakfast: 3 eggs + 2 toast + laban + olives
  • 🏋️ Lunch (pre-gym): Chicken + rice + salad (generous)
  • ⚡ Post-workout: Whey + banana (or chicken sandwich)
  • 🌙 Dinner: Fish/beef + veg + ¼ plate rice
  • 🛌 Before bed: Cup of yogurt / روب

🛌 Rest Day (Tue/Thu/Sat/Sun)

  • ☀️ Breakfast: 3 eggs + 1 toast + veg + laban
  • 🥗 Lunch: Chicken + salad (skip rice or ¼ plate)
  • 🌙 Dinner: Fish/meat + veg only (no carbs)
  • 🛌 Before bed: Yogurt / روب
